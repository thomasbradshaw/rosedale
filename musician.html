Substitute Musician - Piano

Rosedale Church is looking for a substitute to play for worship service every 6-8 weeks. Attendance is 30-40 people.
Piano is a Technics SX-PR602 88-key digital keyboard, with a Kimball 5'2" baby grand as backup.

General Responsibilities

  • 3-4 Hymns
  • Prelude, Offertory, Postlude pieces
  • Incidental Music as needed (communion, interludes, etc.)
  • Sample Bulletin - April 30, 2017 (MSWord 39K)

Current Musical Style

Best described as "blended" with a mix of Classical, Contemporary, Gospel, New Age, Jazz & Pop. Drummer & fiddler will play along ad-hoc.

Minimum Proficiency

  • J.S. Bach Well Tempered Clavier Book 1 level or higher
  • Ability to read down a lead-sheet

Helpful

  • Prior church-playing experience a bonus
  • Ability to expand/contract music as needed to fill allotted time
  • Good opportunity to "let loose"

Pay

  • $100 / Sunday

Send email to uccrosedale@gmail.com with your name & phone number along with a brief description of qualifications.
