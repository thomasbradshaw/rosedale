An Invitation

Whoever you are and wherever you are on life's journey, you're welcome here.

At Rosedale Congregational Church you will find a warm and inclusive community where sharing God's love is at the heart of everything we do. Through prayer and music, scripture and tradition we seek God's guidance and direction in our lives. Through questions and conversation, acceptance and encouragement we begin to discover our gifts and to grow in faithfulness. As a Christian congregation, we are called to follow Jesus in concrete actions of compassion and caring. And we rely on his continuing presence to lead and inspire us as we venture forward.

We are an Open and Affirming church, actively welcoming persons of all sexual orientations and gender identities. We would consider it a privilege to welcome you at Rosedale Congregational Church. We hope that you will join us soon.

Peace,

Rev. Ruth Wagner Bradshaw
