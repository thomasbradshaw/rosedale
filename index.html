An Invitation

We strive to be a place where the healing power of love meets the brokenness of the world. As a Christian congregation, we are called to follow Jesus is concrete actions of compassion and caring.

Through prayer and music, scripture and tradition, we seek to God’s guidance and the living presence of Christ. Through questions and conversation, acceptance and encouragement we begin to discover our gifts and to grow in faithfulness. Whoever you are and wherever you are on life’s journey, you are welcome here. We would consider it a privilege to welcome you at Rosedale Congregational Church.

We hope that you will join us soon.

Peace,

Rev. Ruth Wagner Bradshaw
